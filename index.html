About Me

Currently, I am summer intern at Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore, where I am doing research on how to allow seamless interaction between users and smart-objects in Internet of Things environment.

I received MS (By Research) in Computer Science from Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur and B.Tech in Information Technology from West Bengal University of Technology, Kolkata.

My area of research interest is Human Computer Interaction (HCI), mainly focusing on creating and prototyping novel interaction techniques that empower people's interactive experiences with computers and devices. This research often lies in emerging use modalities, such as wearable computing, touch interfaces and gestural interaction.

For more details, please refer to my CV.
